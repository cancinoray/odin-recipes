Adobo

Description:

Chicken Adobo is a type of Filipino chicken stew. Chicken pieces are marinated in soy sauce and spices, pan-fried, and stewed until tender.
The dish gained popularity because of its delicious taste and ease in preparation.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • ⅔ cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook chicken pieces until golden brown on both sides, then remove.
  3. Stir in the onion and garlic; cook until they soften and brown, about 6 minutes.
  4. Pour in vinegar and soy sauce, and season with garlic powder, black pepper, and bay leaf.
  5. Add the browned chicken, increase the heat to high, and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover.
  7. Simmer until the chicken is tender and cooked through, 35 to 40 minutes.
